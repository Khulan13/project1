OUR STORY

Our incredibly rare beans come from humble beginning in Yemen, where decades of political turmoil once forced local farmers to start growing Khat, a narcotic native to the Arabican Peninsula.

The Dawoodi Bohra Community changed this, removing all the Khat plants and replacing them with coffee, bringing this humble brew back to its roots.
